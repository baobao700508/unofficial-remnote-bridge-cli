# Referring Friends to RemNote

If you know someone who would benefit from better note-taking or knowledge organization, you can send them a personalized invitation to RemNote containing your referral link (containing a unique ID for your account). If they sign up for RemNote using this referral link and start using it, both of you will earn a free month of RemNote Pro!

Free months can only be applied towards **RemNote Pro subscriptions** (monthly or yearly). They cannot be used to purchase the [AI plan](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/6084981-remnote-pro-frequently-asked-questions#h_20d1e935d2), additional AI credits, lifetime Life-Long Learner licenses, or any other products RemNote might offer.

# Sending invitations

There are three ways to send an invitation. All of them can be found in _Settings > Invite_. (The blue bar at the top will only appear once you've earned at least one free month, so don't worry if you don't see it there yet.)

## Invite by sending a link

You can copy your referral link directly from the “Share invite link” box and paste it into an email or text message you write yourself or add it to a social media post.

## Invite by email

You can type your friend’s email address into the _Invite via email_ box and click the _Invite_ button. This will send them a preformatted email (including your referral link) that invites them to start using RemNote.

## Invite by tweeting

If you're active on X/Twitter, you can invite your followers by editing the message as desired and clicking on the _Send Tweet_ option. Even if you don’t know your followers, if they decide to sign up, that’s free RemNote Pro for both of you. And the word spreads about what RemNote can offer learners around the world!

# How to use your free months

Once you have successfully referred a new user, both your accounts will be credited for one free month of RemNote Pro. To prevent abuse, a complete referral requires both of you to verify your email addresses by clicking on the link we send you. (If you've lost the initial email, you'll find a link to resend the verification email in _Settings > Invite_.) **If you're not seeing credit appear for a referral, please check this first** – 90% of apparently missing free months are due to unverified email addresses.

To redeem free months, you need to sign up for a RemNote Pro account and provide a payment method. Credits are always applied before your payment method on file is charged, so if you have enough credit to cover the subscription duration you choose, you won’t be charged at all.

If you already have an active RemNote Pro subscription when you earn referral credit, you don’t need to do anything: your credit will be applied before charging your payment method the next time your subscription renews.

## How to sign up for RemNote Pro

In _Settings > Plans_, choose the plan you want to switch to (in this case, monthly or yearly RemNote Pro; AI plans and lifetime/Life-Long Learner licenses are not eligible, though you can still apply free months towards the Pro part of a Pro+AI plan, and will thus pay only $10/month for a Pro+AI plan). Click the _Subscribe_ button. You’ll be asked to enter payment information. If your referral credit covers the subscription you choose, you will see a $0 charge (if you see a larger charge than you expect, check whether you accidentally selected an annual subscription when you wanted a monthly one). Then you’ll be upgraded!

You can cancel and switch back to the free version from _Settings > Plans_ whenever you want ([see frequently asked questions](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/6084981-remnote-pro-frequently-asked-questions#h_030765f615)). If financial concerns are holding you back, don’t forget that RemNote offers [student discounts and local pricing discounts](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/6084981-remnote-pro-frequently-asked-questions#h_33509a94c9).
