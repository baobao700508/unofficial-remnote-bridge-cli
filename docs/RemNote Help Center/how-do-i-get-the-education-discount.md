# How do I get the Education Discount?

All currently enrolled students, faculty or staff members of an academic institution are eligible for our 25% education discount on **RemNote Pro subscriptions** when using our standard pricing (see the [section at the bottom of the page](#h_4179de0aa8) for notes on non-standard pricing). The education discount is not applicable for the Life-Long Learner license or when buying the AI plan or additional AI credits.

If you are _using your official institution-provided email address_ for your RemNote account, chances are that RemNote already knows you belong to an educational institution, and you'll receive the discount when you subscribe.

# **Changing your personal email to an institutional email**

If you have an institution-provided email address but are not currently using that email to log in to RemNote, follow the steps below to change it.

1.  Go to _Settings > Profile._
    
    [![Profile Settings page. Here you can change your email among other things.](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/akxf7g7x/1248067395/110a780b138216ea6b9cdca20268/image.png?expires=1772827200&signature=14c5c72654db360c5d264c5bdd4f0a2d01ece953df3d498bebb9cba960384835&req=dSIjHsl4moJWXPMW1HO4zWtUiRW8d3Jsdh%2Fdqj%2BSqoKFtF7aKtgJQXGGubAK%0AgGJB%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/akxf7g7x/1248067395/110a780b138216ea6b9cdca20268/image.png?expires=1772827200&signature=14c5c72654db360c5d264c5bdd4f0a2d01ece953df3d498bebb9cba960384835&req=dSIjHsl4moJWXPMW1HO4zWtUiRW8d3Jsdh%2Fdqj%2BSqoKFtF7aKtgJQXGGubAK%0AgGJB%0A)
    
2.  Check the email you're using under _Email Address_. If it's not your institutional email, select "Change Email" and follow the steps to change it.
    

Once that's done, if your institution is part of our database, the discount will automatically apply to your plan and you'll see a discounted price in _Settings > Plans._ The vast majority of institutions across the world are in our database already!

## My institution isn't recognized

If you still don't see a reduced price, most likely our system does not recognize your institution. You can apply for us to add it using [this form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSc0vWCi-8-beIJanzeozfCEkaYnv3QLIP3H0FoSGQKKnZLugA/viewform?usp=sf_link). Our team will review your application and you and your colleagues that use the same email domain will begin receiving the discount when it is approved.

## Notes on discount eligibility

Even as a student, teacher or researcher, there are two special situations that may affect your eligibility for the education discount:

-   Users who purchased RemNote before January 1, 2023 have our legacy Early Bird pricing.
    
-   The education discount and [location-specific pricing discounts](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/6084981-remnote-pro-frequently-asked-questions#h_33509a94c9) **cannot be combined**. If you're eligible for a location-based discount, that will be applied instead; and in every case, the location-based price is equivalent to or lower than the education discount price.
    
-   The discount applies to **RemNote Pro** only. It is not available on the [Life-Long Learner](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/7000659-what-s-included-in-the-life-long-learner-plan) plan or [AI credits](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/6084981-remnote-pro-frequently-asked-questions#h_33509a94c9:~:text=If%20you%20have%20an%20AI%20plan%2C%20any%20discounts%20you%27re%20eligible%20for%20are%20not%20applied%20to%20the%20part%20of%20the%20plan%20that%20pays%20for%20AI%20credits%20\(otherwise%20we%20would%20be%20selling%20them%20at%20a%20loss\).).
    
-   If you subscribe through the **iOS App Store or Google Play**, discounts cannot be applied. To use the education discount, subscribe via the web app at [remnote.com](https://remnote.com). See [Subscribing to RemNote on Mobile Devices](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/7225486-subscribing-to-remnote-on-mobile-devices) for details.
