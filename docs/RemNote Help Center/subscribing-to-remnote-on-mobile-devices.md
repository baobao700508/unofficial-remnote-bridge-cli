# Subscribing to RemNote on Mobile Devices

# Subscribing via the mobile app

Simply navigate to the _Settings > Plans_ section within the RemNote mobile app on your iOS or Android device and follow the prompts to purchase RemNote Pro!

## Important note for iOS and Android users: Discounts and free months received through the referral program

If you subscribe to RemNote Pro through the Apple App Store on iOS or the Google Play Store on Android, please be aware of important limitations due to the platforms’ policies:

-   **Discounts cannot be applied** to subscriptions initiated through the App Store.
    

-   **Free months of RemNote Pro** earned through [our referral program](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/6751988-referring-friends-to-remnote) (either by inviting friends or being invited) cannot be redeemed on subscriptions managed through the App Store or Google Play.
    

If you want to apply a discount or free months, you'll first need to switch to a standard subscription (see next section).

# Switching to a standard subscription

If you subscribed using the iOS app and want to switch to the standard web billing method – perhaps because you want to use a discount (like [the education discount](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/6813476-how-do-i-get-the-education-discount) or free referral months) or you're getting rid of your iOS device – you can do so as follows:

1.  **Open RemNote** on a desktop computer (using either the web app or the desktop app), or log in to RemNote using your mobile browser (such as Chrome or Safari) by visiting [https://remnote.com](https://remnote.com/).
    
2.  Once logged in, go to _Settings > Plans_ and follow the on-screen instructions to guide you through the process (takes just a few minutes).
    

# All subscriptions work across all devices

No matter how you subscribe (mobile app, web app, or desktop app), your RemNote Pro subscription gives you access on an unlimited number of devices. Just log in with your account and your Pro benefits will be active everywhere!
