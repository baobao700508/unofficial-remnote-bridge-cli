# How can I pay?

To purchase RemNote Pro or Life-Long Learning or apply account credit for access to RemNote Pro, you can attach one of the following payment methods to your account in _Settings > Plans_.

You can find more information about how subscriptions work [here](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/6084981-remnote-pro-frequently-asked-questions).

# What payment methods are supported?

When the method involves an app or service, a link is provided to its home page or setup guide.

Please note that some options listed here may not be available in your country, and some options are only available for Life-Long Learning purchases, rather than ongoing subscriptions. If an option shown here is not offered at checkout, then it's likely not available for one of those reasons.

**1\. Credit and debit cards** (most major card issuers)

**2\. Wallets:**

-   [Apple Pay](https://www.apple.com/apple-pay/)
    
-   [Google Pay](https://support.google.com/pay/india/answer/7293696?hl=en&co=GENIE.Platform%3DAndroid)
    
-   [Cash App Pay](https://cash.app)
    
-   [Alipay](https://www.alipay.com/)
    
-   [WeChat Pay](https://pay.weixin.qq.com/index.php/public/wechatpay)
    
-   Stripe's [Link](https://link.com/)
    

**3\. Buy now, pay later services:**

-   [Klarna](https://www.klarna.com/)
    
-   [Affirm](https://www.affirm.com/)
    
-   [Afterpay](https://www.afterpay.com/) ([Clearpay in UK](https://www.clearpay.co.uk/))
    

**4\. Bank debits:**

-   [SEPA Direct Debit](https://www.google.com/search?q=how+does+SEPA+work+with+my+bank%3F&sxsrf=APwXEdeHVWBHMi6fN9xGlmFaQ2mi_ZKl4g%3A1682031735872&ei=d8RBZO7rNLG40PEP6KiPgA0&ved=0ahUKEwiug_jAyLn-AhUxHDQIHWjUA9AQ4dUDCBA&uact=5&oq=how+does+SEPA+work+with+my+bank%3F&gs_lcp=Cgxnd3Mtd2l6LXNlcnAQAzIFCCEQoAEyBQghEKABOgoIABBHENYEELADOgQIIxAnOhAIABCABBAUEIcCELEDEIMBOgoIABCABBAUEIcCOggIABCABBCxAzoLCC4QgAQQxwEQrwE6BQgAEIAEOggIABCKBRCRAjoGCAAQFhAeOggIABCKBRCGAzoFCCEQqwI6CwghEBYQHhAdEIsDOggIIRCgARCLAzoICCEQFhAeEB06CAghEKsCEIsDSgQIQRgAULIDWKUuYPYvaAJwAXgBgAHCAogB8yeSAQgwLjE5LjkuMZgBAKABAcgBCLgBA8ABAQ&sclient=gws-wiz-serp#bsht=CgRmYnNtEgQIBDAB)
    

We don't support methods that are not on this list; for example, we don't support PayPal.

_Note:_ SEPA Direct Debit only works for countries in the Single Euro Payments Area, and only when paying in euros. Since [Life-Long Learning](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/7000659-what-s-included-in-the-life-long-learner-plan) licenses are always purchased in USD, it is not an option for such licenses.

## Where is my credit card data stored?

RemNote Pro purchases are processed by [Stripe](https://stripe.com), a third-party payment processor used by millions of internet companies. RemNote does not store or have access to your credit card number or other sensitive payment details, whatever payment option you choose.
