# Using Quick Navigation Routes

RemNote provides a set of direct routes and URLs that let you instantly access key areas of your knowledge base, documents, and study tools.

Using these quick navigation routes, you can streamline your workflow and jump straight to the content you need—for example, by pinning or bookmarking these URLs in your web browser and opening the desired pages in just one click.

# Quick navigation routes

**1\. Today’s Daily Document**

-   **Route:** [remnote.com/today ↗](https://www.remnote.com/today)  
    Opens the [Daily Document](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/6752031-daily-documents) for the current date.
    

**2\. All Notes**

-   **Route:** [remnote.com‎⁠/notes⁠](https://remnote.com/notes) [↗](https://www.remnote.com/today)  
    Opens the All Notes page, where you can manage every Rem in your knowledge base.
    

**3\. Global Flashcard Queue**

-   **Route:** ‎⁠[remnote.com/flashcards ↗  
    ​](https://remnote.com/flashcards)Opens your global flashcard queue for your entire knowledge base.
    

**4\. Flashcard Queue for a Specific Document**

-   **Route Format:** ‎⁠`/flashcards/{doc_id}⁠`  
    Opens the flashcard queue for a specific document. Replace ‎⁠`{doc_id}`⁠ with the document’s unique ID (visible in your browser's address bar when you open that document in the web version of RemNote).  
    ​  
    ​_Example:_ ‎⁠`https://remnote.com/flashcards/abc123⁠`
    

**5\. Flashcards Home**

-   **Route:** ‎⁠[remnote.com/flashcards\_home⁠ ↗](https://remnote.com/flashcards_home)  
    Opens the [Flashcards Home](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/7925835-the-flashcard-home) page for managing and reviewing your flashcard sets.
    

**6\. Cards Table**

-   **Route:** [remnote.com/cards ↗](https://www.remnote.com/cards)  
    Opens the Card Table, where you can view and manage all your flashcards in a table format.
    

**7\. Import Page**

-   **Route:** ‎[remnote.com/import ↗](https://www.remnote.com/import)  
    Opens the [Import page](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/7898005-importing-notes) to bring in notes, documents, or data from other sources.
    

**8\. Open Rem in Editor by ID**

-   **Route Format:** `‎⁠/w/{rem_id}`⁠  
    Opens a specific Rem directly in the editor by its ID. You can find the ID in your browser's address bar when you [zoom in to](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/6030710-zooming-in-to-rems) the Rem.  
    ​  
    ​_Example:_ ‎⁠`https://remnote.com/w/xyz789⁠`
    

**9\. MCAT Course Page**

-   **Route:** [remnote.com/mcat ↗](https://www.remnote.com/mcat)  
    Opens the MCAT course page.
