# My Payment Failed

In some cases RemNote will not be able to bill your credit card, preventing you from buying the pro subscription.

Please take these steps to fix this issue:

1.  Please confirm with your bank that your credit card supports foreign/online payment, or
    
2.  Try using another credit card. [See more about payment methods here](https://intercom.help/remnote/en/articles/7546279-how-can-i-pay).
    
3.  If you don’t have another credit card, you can also consider using a prepaid debit card. You can likely find such cards in a local convenience store.
    

If none of these work, please contact support.
