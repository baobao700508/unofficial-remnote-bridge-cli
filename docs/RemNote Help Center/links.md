# Links

Links are like [Rem References](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/6030714-rem-references), but they can have any text – they don’t always show the same text as the Rem they link to. You can link either to a Rem in your knowledge base or to a website. Links, unlike Rem References, are underlined when looking at your notes.

# Creating links

Type or find the section of text you wish the link to be attached to, then select it and click the _Link_ button or press Ctrl+Alt+K (Cmd+Opt+K on a Mac).

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/akxf7g7x/2085498640/5187cf9a8862f1abdd3e4969e905/CleanShot+2026-02-20+at+11_20_02%402x.png?expires=1772827200&signature=5bc677b11a779827578baa22de8b9c0dc1010437b69cf09be232a82a392319a8&req=diAvE813lYdbWfMW1HO4zVDvmI2D1UAchDnID2BgVOd39%2FlTYcxdLFCfhviS%0AqI9ek1IFzBDcV571wDg%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/akxf7g7x/2085498640/5187cf9a8862f1abdd3e4969e905/CleanShot+2026-02-20+at+11_20_02%402x.png?expires=1772827200&signature=5bc677b11a779827578baa22de8b9c0dc1010437b69cf09be232a82a392319a8&req=diAvE813lYdbWfMW1HO4zVDvmI2D1UAchDnID2BgVOd39%2FlTYcxdLFCfhviS%0AqI9ek1IFzBDcV571wDg%3D%0A)

For a link to a web page, paste the URL and select the format in which you want to add this link:

-   **Formatted Link:** The link is automatically attached to a title of the website, making it clickable, but the URL itself is hidden. If you want your writing to look polished and easy to read, formatted links are usually preferred.
    
-   **Text Link:** The actual URL (web address) appears directly in the text.
    

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/akxf7g7x/1682757599/5db2d87820d84e7f8e40f13fb4de/image.png?expires=1772827200&signature=587f53619b5250615001cfc1466b540c1329e542ae8b0944037ae53d58d98ced&req=dSYvFM57moRWUPMW1HO4zZVyVEFrBG6cMMENYV%2FjjrFRX7tquRokb2ahF2wJ%0ABoyCOMLQMqt6LUzjbAM%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/akxf7g7x/1682757599/5db2d87820d84e7f8e40f13fb4de/image.png?expires=1772827200&signature=587f53619b5250615001cfc1466b540c1329e542ae8b0944037ae53d58d98ced&req=dSYvFM57moRWUPMW1HO4zZVyVEFrBG6cMMENYV%2FjjrFRX7tquRokb2ahF2wJ%0ABoyCOMLQMqt6LUzjbAM%3D%0A)

-   For a link to a Rem in your Knowledge Base, start typing to search your Knowledge Base.
    

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/akxf7g7x/1682792967/b80f7c7026574822a60407fae26f/CleanShot+2025-08-22+at+14_40_35.gif?expires=1772827200&signature=210d27a1b4d13d12582238e62468ca4c25a45eb635097b6ff815f1e6f9a489dc&req=dSYvFM53n4hZXvMW1HO4zYSuleSfS5BQxxGUlO5cGhUKwQDhErR77Z0zH%2BE8%0AeJ2oc%2FjUUo0AwnkZkeI%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/akxf7g7x/1682792967/b80f7c7026574822a60407fae26f/CleanShot+2025-08-22+at+14_40_35.gif?expires=1772827200&signature=210d27a1b4d13d12582238e62468ca4c25a45eb635097b6ff815f1e6f9a489dc&req=dSYvFM53n4hZXvMW1HO4zYSuleSfS5BQxxGUlO5cGhUKwQDhErR77Z0zH%2BE8%0AeJ2oc%2FjUUo0AwnkZkeI%3D%0A)

Clicking on the link will navigate to the website or Rem.

If your link is to a Rem, it will create a [backlink](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/6030776-backlinks) and affect [flashcard gathering](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/8892109-how-does-remnote-decide-what-flashcards-are-part-of-a-document), the same way as it would if you had created a Reference to that Rem.

# Working with links

If you left-click on a link, the target website or Rem opens immediately. If you right-click, you’ll see several other options:

-   _Edit link_: lets you update the link title or change the URL that opens when you click the link.
    
-   _Open Externally:_ opens the link in a new browser tab, just like a left-click
    
-   _Copy link_: copies the link to your clipboard.
    
-   _Turn into Inline/_Formatted _Link_: lets you switch between different link display styles in your document.  
    See the “Creating links” section above for more details about text and formatted links.

# Deeplinks

A **[deeplink](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deep_linking)** is a URL that points directly to a specific Rem in your Knowledge Base. You can use deeplinks to quickly navigate to a particular Rem from outside RemNote — for example, from a bookmark in your browser, a link in another app, or a note you've shared with someone.

Note: Deeplinks require the [RemNote desktop app](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/6030835-desktop-app). Clicking a deeplink will open the Rem directly in the desktop app.

## Getting a Deeplink

To get the deeplink for any Rem, start typing `/deeplink` command and select _Copy Deeplink_. You can then paste this URL wherever you need it. You can also assign a custom [keyboard shortcut](https://help.remnote.com/en/articles/7893440-keyboard-shortcuts) to this action for even faster access.

## Workflow Example

Let's say you're a student who uses the Notes app to plan your day. You've got a list of things to do — attend lectures, finish assignments, review notes.

Instead of switching to RemNote and searching for the right document each time, you can paste deeplinks to your lecture notes right into your daily plan. When it's time to review, just click the link and the RemNote desktop app opens the exact note instantly.

## Will my deeplink break if I rename or move a Rem?

No. Deeplinks are bound to the Rem's unique ID, not its name or location. You can freely rename a Rem, edit its text, or move it anywhere in your Knowledge Base — your deeplink will keep working.
